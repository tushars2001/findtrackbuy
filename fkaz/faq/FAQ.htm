What is FindTrackBuy.com?

FindTrackBuy.com is free analytical tool that helps you save money by keeping track of products you wish to buy and alert you via email when price drops.

How FindTrackBuy saves me money?

There are various way this tool can help you save money, below are two examples:

  1. You track the product that you want to buy and we will inform you when price drops to certain levels as per you.
  2. Before buying product online, just check its history. May be the product that you are buying is high at cost and might come down. So track it and save bucks.

Why I see graph with only few days or sometime just blank graph?

Amazon/Flipkart has millions of products. We put many products daily in our system to track their price history. Or whatever our users search/track, it becomes part of tracking system. So if the product you are looking for was put in our system a few days before, there will be lesser history which will keep on growing everyday. So if you search for a product with less or no data, no worries, track it and we will alert you when price drops.

I saw discrepency in price that I see on FindTrackBuy versus Actual site?

In rare circumstances, you may see discrepency in prices on our site and Amazon/Flipkart. We track our products daily once or twice. So if we track price in morning, and in afternoon price was changed, then you may see different price, but in night, when we track price again, it will be matching to Actual product at Amazon/Flipkart. So prices in our site are not older then a day. Also, you can report any issue to us by 'Report Issue' option or sending us email at: Find.Track.Buy@QuirkyCrackers.com
